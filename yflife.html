Exciting Partnership Announcement:
AI-Powered HR Solution Chosen by YF Life Insurance
International Limited

We are thrilled that our company has been selected by YF Life Insurance International Limited (“YF”),
a premier provider of employee medical benefits and pension schemes, to offer our cutting-edge AI-powered
HR solution for its existing and future clients in Hong Kong and Macau.

After a thorough evaluation of the companies in the Park by the Hong Kong Science & Technology
Park Company, our comprehensive AI-powered HR was recommended to YF, who highly appraised its
capabilities and functionalities.

The signing ceremony with YF will be organized by YF and HKSTP jointly in July 2024, further
solidifying this exciting collaboration. This partnership represents a significant milestone for our
organization, our branding, recognition and industry standing. It also reflects the trust and
confidence placed in our innovative technology and our commitment to delivering exceptional
AI-powered HR services.

We are excited to embark on this new chapter and are grateful for the support and trust of our
clients, colleagues, and invaluable friends like you.

K.C. Sit
Managing Director
